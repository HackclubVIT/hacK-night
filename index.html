About

HackClub’s flagship hackathon hacKnight, is a 48-hour long hackathon with a jaw dropping cash prize pool of Rs. 1,90,000. Assemble your tech-savvy avengers and get ready to compete in VIT Chennai’s biggest in-person Hackathon taking place on 11th April.

It is a perfect platform to learn, develop, compete, network, and take home cash, all under 1 roof.

Get ready to design, develop, decentralise, and win free event T-shirts, have luscious food and vibe along for 2 days!

Event details

  • Date: 11th April - 13th April
  • Time: 11th April 5:30pm to 13th April 7:30pm
  • Entry Fee: 200 per person
  • Team Size: 3-4 Members
  • Venue: MG Auditorium
Guidelines


  • The participants are required to gather at the aforementioned venue before 6:00pm with their VIT ID cards.
  • We will issue event-specific ID cards to all participants and assign HackClub’s representatives to all the teams.
  • The opening ceremony will commence at 6:30pm, the domains will be announced.
  • The hackathon will commence at 7:00 pm IST on 11th April. There will be a total of 2 rounds.
  • The final results will be announced at 7:30pm IST on 13th April.
  • You are not allowed to leave the hackathon venue until either you get eliminated or in an event of emergency.
  • On-duty leave (OD) will be provided to all the participants for Tuesday and Wednesday.
  • Only VIT Chennai students can register for the event.
  • Leaving the hackathon premises without permit will cause direct disqualification of the team.
Rounds and Rules


There will be a total of 2 rounds.

  1. All the rounds are elimination rounds, implying some teams advance to the next round, some unfortunately return back with loads of experience.
    • Review-1: Review-1 will commence on 12th April 10:00 am IST. Approximately 45 teams proceed to the next round. All the participants moving to round-2 will receive free T-Shirts.
    • Review-2: Review-2 will commence on 13th April 5:00 pm IST. No elimination will take place after Round-1.

FAQ

Do I need to have prior development knowledge to participate?

HackClub will have its Tech team to support all the participants 24x7, apart from that having prior coding/ development experience will give you an upperhand.

How long will the Hackathon last?

The hackathon will last for approximately 48 hours, we will provide all the participants with food, wifi, T-shirts (after Review-1)

What are the Hackathon Domains/ problem statements?

The problem statements/ Domains will be announced during the opening ceremony of hacKnight 2022 (11th April)

What if I don’t reach the final round?

You still get a 48-hour long learning experience, network with your peers, get a cool T-shirt , and get mentored by SMEs and Industry Experts.

What do I need to bring along?

An enthusiastic team, your coding setup (Laptop, charger, keyboard etc) and, we’ll take care of the rest. HackClub will provide WiFi, food (3 meals a day), and other basic necessities.

How many elimination rounds will be there?

Round-1 is the elimination round, 50% teams will proceed to the next round. Round-2 will be the final round
